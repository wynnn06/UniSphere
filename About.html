Purpose

Unisphere is for PUP students
to find accredited or
revalidated campus
organizations in one platform.

Functionality

Unisphere supports full CRUD
operations for managing org
records efficiently.

Student Empowerment

Unisphere helps students
discover orgs that match
their goals and interests.

Your Gateway to Campus Involvement.

Technical Design

The system combines a
structured database with a
user-friendly interface.

Sorting System

Organizations are sorted
alphabetically using the
Quick Sort algorithm.

Search Algorithm

Unisphere uses Linear Search to
quickly find student
organizations.
